About Us

BIMATRIX is an emerging BIM consultancy

A technology solutions provider dedicated to transforming the built environment across the United Arab Emirates and the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC).

We specialize in delivering advanced BIM services that empower architects, engineers, contractors, and owners to optimize design, reduce risk, improve project coordination, and enhance lifecycle asset management.

With deep local understanding and global best practices, we support public and private sector clients across commercial, infrastructure, industrial, residential, and hospitality sectors.

Our Services

Mission

To enable smarter, more efficient construction and operations through digital innovation — bridging technology and industry expertise to deliver measurable value on every project.

Vision

To empower AEC stakeholders globally with affordable BIM and construction documentation services that reduce risk, improve collaboration, and ensure buildable outcomes.

Values

To deliver smart, coordinated BIM solutions for the global AEC industry, making Virtual Design & Construction accessible to all.

BIM Services

Coordination

MEP Coordinated BIM Model

A fully integrated 3D model where Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing systems are developed, reviewed, and coordinated with Architectural and Structural models to eliminate clashes and ensure smooth construction — critical for MEP-intensive UAE/GCC projects.

Clash Detection

Navisworks Clash-Free BIM Model

A coordinated, federated model where Architectural, Structural, and MEP disciplines are tested, reviewed, and resolved using Autodesk Navisworks Manage, ensuring construction-ready, interference-free designs — essential for MEP-heavy UAE/GCC projects.
